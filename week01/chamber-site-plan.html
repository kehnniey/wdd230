Springfield Chamber of Commerce - Site Plan

Site Name

Springfield Chamber of Commerce - This name represents the local business community of Springfield, aiming to support economic growth and networking.

Site Purpose

  • Provide resources and networking opportunities for local businesses.
  • Promote economic growth through community events and business development programs.

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in the area?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #2C3E50 (Used for headers and navigation background)

Secondary Color: #E67E22 (Used for accents like buttons and links)

Typography

Headings: 'Roboto', sans-serif

Body Text: 'Open Sans', sans-serif
