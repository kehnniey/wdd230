Springfield Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Plan Overview

Site Name

Springfield Chamber of Commerce
Represents the Springfield business community and aims to support local economic development and networking.

Site Purpose

  • Provide resources, directories, and support for local businesses and entrepreneurs.
  • Promote local events and foster business-to-business and community engagement.

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber host this month that promote business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the Chamber Management?
  • What are the latest demographics and population trends in Springfield?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #2C3E50 (Header, navigation bar)
  • Secondary Color: #E67E22 (Buttons, highlights)
  • Accent Color: #F4F4F4 (Backgrounds)

Typography

  • Headings: Roboto, sans-serif
  • Body Text: Open Sans, sans-serif
